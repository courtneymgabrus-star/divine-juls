About Juls

the science of healing
meets the wisdom of the body.

"I came to this work because I needed it. I stayed because my community needed it too."

As a queer woman with a doctorate in public health, Julianna spent years studying health disparities on paper before realizing that the most transformative healing happens in the body — in breath, in sound, in intentional community.

Based in Atlanta, Georgia, she weaves together evidence-based public health practice with yoga, sound healing, and ritual — creating spaces where LGBTQIA+ folks and all who seek intentional, grounded healing can finally feel at home.

Work with Juls

Yoga

where she began —
and keeps returning.

Julianna is a 200-hour certified yoga teacher devoted to creating inclusive, trauma-informed, safe spaces for others to turn inward and connect their mind, body, and breath.

Drawing from yin and restorative traditions, her classes offer space for grounding, softness, choice, and reflection — no prior experience needed, no performance required.

Yoga came first. It's the foundation everything else is rooted in.

Sound Healing

let the bowls
do the talking.

Crystal singing bowls produce pure tones that resonate with the body's own frequency — quieting the nervous system, releasing held tension, and creating space for deep rest and integration.

Each bowl is tuned to a different note and chakra. Together they create a full-body sound bath experience — you don't listen to it so much as you become it.

Format Private sessions · group sound baths · retreats
Length 45 – 90 minutes
